4. Key Security Features (Explained)

  • HTTPS Only: Prevents interception of data.
  • Strong Authentication: MFA, secure passwords.
  • Role-Based Access: Students, staff, admin.
  • Data Minimization: Only collect required info.
  • Audit Logs: Track who accessed student data.
  • Encryption: Protect data in transit and at rest.
